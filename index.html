Nova 4WD Robotic Platform

This powerful all-terrain robotic chassis is a heavy-duty 4-wheel drive mobile platform designed for demanding outdoor environments. The chassis measures 26 inches long, 20 inches wide, with a 13-inch ground clearance and 15-inch diameter wheels, it offers exceptional off-road capability on rough fields, mud, and uneven terrain. Weighing only 35kg, the platform is powered by four 350W high-torque motors and dual 36V batteries, enabling it to carry over 250kg and pull loads up to 3 tons. Built for durability and performance, it serves as an ideal base for firefighting robots, agricultural systems, surveillance, and various heavy-duty robotic applications.
